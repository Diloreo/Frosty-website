Inspiring generations to generations

with the finest organic Ice cream in the town...



Frosty brand was invented by three Sri Lankan entreprenuers. These three individuals worked at a five-star hotel in Colombo for 5 years. One day, while they were working together for an ice cream fest hosted by their hotel, they came up with an incredible idea "Why don't we make our own organic ice cream brand?". Ever since then, their dream was to make their very own ice cream brand.
In 2020, they took the initial steps to make their own brand and in 2021 they officially started their own ice cream brand, opening their factory in Colombo 03, transforming their dream into a reality. By transforming the finest ingrediants into rich and indulgent ice cream, they created the first and luxury ice cream. Initially they started off with 3 flavours Vainilla espanola, Belgian coco and St' Berry. Although the brand "Frosty" to the market, our flavours used to fascinate people and their tastebuds and unexpectedly, we started getting a very high demand towards our products.



Who we are now...


Due to the highe demand in the market, today, Frosty has expanded its range of taste buds by opening a branch in Galle, allowing many other tongues to experience these premium organic ice cream. Earlier we started off with 5 workers and today we have nearly 45 staff members along with a wide variety of tastes with 9 different and unique flavours. Our vision is to bring the finest and the premium organic ice cream to your tastebuds.
